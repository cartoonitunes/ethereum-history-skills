# Supporting EthereumHistory

EthereumHistory is a free, open archive with no ads, no paywalls, and no token gating. Maintaining it requires time, hosting, and research tooling.

## Donate

- **ETH / USDC:** `0x123bf3b32fB3986C9251C81430d2542D5054F0d2`
- **ENS:** `ethereumhistory.eth`
- **Donate page:** [ethereumhistory.com/donate](https://ethereumhistory.com/donate)

## What Donations Fund

- Server and database hosting
- Compiler archaeology tooling (building historical C++ solc, Serpent environments)
- Research infrastructure (archival APIs, Wayback Machine access)
- Development time for new features

## Other Ways to Help

- **Contribute history** — document a contract you know about
- **Submit a bytecode proof** — crack an unverified contract
- **Spread the word** — link to contract pages when you reference early Ethereum history
- **Star the repo** — helps with discoverability

Thank you for helping preserve Ethereum history.
